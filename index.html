About Vexon

Specialized recruitment. Personal service.

Vexon Recruiting is a Canadian recruitment firm focused on skilled trades, industrial maintenance, mining, heavy equipment, manufacturing and power engineering.

Led by owner Jason Blanchard, Vexon Recruiting takes a relationship-first approach with employers and candidates. The goal is straightforward: understand the need, communicate clearly and introduce qualified people who can contribute to safe, productive operations.
