Market Insights

What we’re seeing across Canada’s industrial hiring market.

POWER ENGINEERING

Certification narrows the market quickly

Employers seeking 2nd or 3rd Class Power Engineers often compete for a small pool of candidates with the right plant experience and location flexibility.

HEAVY EQUIPMENT

Rotational schedules remain a key attraction

Qualified technicians frequently compare schedule, travel, camp conditions, overtime and equipment exposure—not hourly rate alone.

MAINTENANCE

Passive candidates require direct outreach

Experienced millwrights, electricians and maintenance leaders are often employed and need a clear reason to consider a move.
