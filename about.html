About Vexon

Specialized recruitment. Personal service.

A focused Canadian recruitment firm.

Vexon Recruiting specializes in skilled trades, industrial maintenance, mining, heavy equipment, manufacturing and power engineering recruitment across Canada.

Led by owner Jason Blanchard, the business takes a relationship-first approach with employers and candidates. The goal is to learn the real requirements behind each role, communicate clearly and present people whose experience and goals align with the opportunity.

Vexon’s aim is not to be the largest recruitment firm. It is to be a trusted, responsive specialist for Canada’s industrial economy.

Specialized Canadian recruitment for skilled trades, industrial maintenance, mining, heavy equipment and power engineering.
