Who We Recruit

Experienced electricians for demanding industrial environments.

We are interested in speaking with qualified Industrial Electricians whose experience aligns with maintenance, manufacturing, mining, marine, utilities, power generation, processing and heavy industrial operations.

Candidate suitability may depend on certification, equipment exposure, industry experience, location, schedule flexibility, work authorization and employer-specific requirements.

Location

Atlantic Canada and Canada-wide

Opportunities may include permanent, site-based, travel, project and rotational work depending on employer demand.

Candidate Profile

Qualifications and experience we look for.

Trade Certification

Red Seal Industrial Electrician certification or an equivalent Canadian-recognized industrial electrical qualification.

Industrial Maintenance

Experience maintaining, repairing and troubleshooting industrial electrical systems and production equipment.

Controls and Automation

Experience with PLC systems, variable-frequency drives, control panels, motor controls and automation equipment.

Electrical Systems

Knowledge of high- and low-voltage systems, distribution, transformers, motors and industrial power equipment.

Technical Documentation

Ability to read electrical drawings, blueprints, schematics, wiring diagrams and technical manuals.

Safety and Compliance

Strong understanding of electrical codes, lockout procedures, workplace safety and industrial operating standards.

Typical Experience

Relevant skills may include:

  • Preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Electrical troubleshooting and diagnostics
  • Motors and motor control centres
  • PLCs and industrial automation
  • Variable-frequency drives
  • Industrial panels and distribution systems
  • Generators and backup power systems
  • Instrumentation and control circuits
  • Hydraulic and pneumatic electrical controls
  • Shutdown and commissioning support
  • Work-order and maintenance systems
  • Blueprint and schematic interpretation
