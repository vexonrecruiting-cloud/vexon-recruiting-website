Who We Recruit

Experienced mechanical tradespeople for industrial operations.

We are interested in speaking with qualified Millwrights and Industrial Mechanics whose experience aligns with mining, manufacturing, processing, forestry, utilities, energy and heavy industrial maintenance.

Candidate suitability may depend on certification, equipment exposure, industry experience, location, schedule flexibility, work authorization and employer-specific requirements.

Location

Canada-wide opportunities

Opportunities may include permanent plant maintenance, field service, shutdown, turnaround, project and rotational work.

Candidate Profile

Qualifications and experience we look for.

Trade Certification

Red Seal Millwright certification or another recognized Canadian Industrial Mechanic qualification.

Industrial Maintenance

Experience maintaining, repairing, installing and troubleshooting industrial machinery and production equipment.

Rotating Equipment

Experience with pumps, compressors, gearboxes, bearings, turbines, fans and other rotating equipment.

Conveyors and Processing Systems

Knowledge of conveyors, crushers, screens, feeders, mills and material-handling systems.

Hydraulics and Pneumatics

Ability to diagnose and repair hydraulic and pneumatic systems, cylinders, valves and controls.

Safety and Compliance

Strong understanding of lockout procedures, confined spaces, work permits and industrial safety standards.

Typical Experience

Relevant skills may include:

  • Preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Mechanical troubleshooting and diagnostics
  • Pumps, compressors and gearboxes
  • Bearings, shafts and couplings
  • Conveyors and material-handling systems
  • Rigging, alignment and equipment installation
  • Hydraulic and pneumatic systems
  • Welding and fabrication support
  • Shutdown and turnaround maintenance
  • Precision measurement and laser alignment
  • CMMS and work-order systems
  • Blueprint and technical drawing interpretation
